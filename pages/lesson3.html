The Secret Sauce: SPAR — The Loop That Makes These Agents Work

Why do these agents feel so effective? Because they don't just follow a script. They operate in a continuous loop called Sense → Plan → Act → Respond. This cycle lets them learn and get better over time — turning a one-time automation into a permanent competitive advantage.

1. SENSEIt sees the trigger — an email, a form submission, a calendar event.
2. PLANIt reasons: "What's the best next step for this specific situation?"
3. ACTIt executes — sends the email, updates the CRM, books the meeting.
4. RESPONDIt logs the outcome and refines its approach. The agent gets smarter.

That last step — Respond — is what makes it "agentic." It's not just a tool; it's a teammate that learns. It's the difference between a static FAQ and a salesperson who remembers what every customer asks — and gets better at converting them every week.
