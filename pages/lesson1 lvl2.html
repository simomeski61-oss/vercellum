🧩 COMPONENTS
THE FOUR BUILDING BLOCKS

Your Sales Agent Has 4 Components — Click Each One to Understand It

Each component is a module your agent uses. Together, they create a seamless pipeline. Individually, each one is simple enough to explain to a ten-year-old. Click any card to see what it does in your business.

📥
1. CAPTURE
How leads enter the pipeline
The Capture module connects to every entry point in your business: your website contact form, your live chat widget, email inquiries, even social media DMs. Whenever someone expresses interest, the agent captures their details — name, contact, company, intent — and opens a lead record automatically.
💡 Real example: A visitor fills your "Get a Quote" form at 11 PM. Within 3 seconds, a lead record is created in your CRM, the agent begins analysing the request, and a personalised acknowledgement is on its way.
🧠
2. QUALIFY
Does this lead fit your ideal customer?
Qualification means determining if a lead is worth pursuing. Your agent uses the criteria you define — budget range, industry, company size, project timeline — and asks targeted follow-up questions or analyses the information already available. It maps every lead against your ideal customer profile.
💡 Real example: A lead mentions "we're looking to start within the month." The agent flags this as time-sensitive and moves it to high-priority immediately — no human review needed.
🏆
3. SCORE
How valuable is this lead?
Scoring converts qualification data into a number — typically 1 to 100 — so your team always knows which leads to call first. Points are assigned based on factors like budget fit, decision-making authority, urgency, and alignment with your services. High scores = human follow-up immediately. Low scores = nurture sequence.
💡 Real example: A lead who mentions a $50K budget, is the CEO, needs the work done this quarter, and matches your target industry perfectly scores 94/100. Your team gets an instant alert.
➡️
4. ROUTE
What happens next — automatically
Routing is what happens after scoring. Hot leads (score 70+) trigger an immediate notification to your sales team and optionally offer a calendar booking link. Warm leads (40–69) enter a personalised email sequence. Cold leads (under 40) go into a long-term nurture track. Every lead gets the right attention — automatically.
💡 Real example: A 94-score lead gets a personal email from your sales rep (drafted by the agent), plus an offer to book a call — all sent while the rep is in a different meeting.
🔨 BUILD STEPS
HOW IT'S BUILT

3 Practical Steps to Build Your Sales Agent — No Code Required

This is how real agents are built for SMBs using modern no-code platforms. Each step has a corresponding prompt you can use directly in platforms like Make.com, HubSpot, or a custom agent builder.

1
SET UP CAPTURE — Connect Your Entry Points
Connect your website form, chat widget, and email inbox to your agent platform. Define what data you need: name, email, company, project description, and budget. Set up a CRM record to be created automatically on every submission.
When a new inquiry arrives from [website form / chat / email], create a CRM record with: name, email, company, inquiry type, and timestamp. Send an immediate acknowledgement: "Thanks [Name] — we've received your enquiry and our team will be in touch within 2 hours." Log the lead status as "New."
2
QUALIFICATION & SCORING — Define Your Ideal Customer
Write down the 3–5 criteria that define a great customer for your business. Budget threshold, decision-making authority, industry fit, timeline urgency. Assign point values to each and configure the scoring logic in your agent.
Analyse this lead against our ideal customer criteria: Budget ≥ $5,000 (+30 pts), Decision-maker contact (+25 pts), Project starts within 90 days (+20 pts), Industry match (+15 pts), Previous engagement (+10 pts). Calculate total score (max 100). If score ≥ 70, tag as HOT. If 40–69, tag as WARM. If <40, tag as COLD.
3
ROUTING — Automate What Happens Next
Map your routing logic: HOT leads trigger immediate sales team notification and a personalised email with a booking link. WARM leads enter a 3-email nurture sequence. COLD leads receive a resources email and are added to a monthly newsletter.
If lead score ≥ 70 (HOT): notify sales team via Slack/email with lead summary, send personalised email with calendar link, update CRM to "Sales Follow-Up Required." If score 40–69 (WARM): enrol in 3-step email sequence, first email within 1 hour. If score <40 (COLD): send helpful resources email, add to newsletter list, set 30-day re-check reminder.

These three prompts — configured in your chosen platform — are the foundation of a fully operational Lead & Sales Agent. The entire setup can be completed in an afternoon.

THREE THINGS TO REMEMBER FOREVER

The 3 Principles Behind Every Effective Sales Agent

01
GOAL = QUALIFICATION, NOT CONVERSATION
Your agent's job isn't to chat — it's to determine quickly whether a lead is worth your team's time. Every question it asks, every email it sends, is designed to reveal one thing: is this person a genuine buyer?
02
SPEED = VALUE. ALWAYS.
The business that responds first wins the deal 78% of the time. Speed is your competitive advantage. An agent that responds in 60 seconds beats a human following up in 2 hours — every single time, without exception.
03
AUTOMATION STARTS WITH ONE RULE
Don't try to automate everything at once. The most effective sales agents started with one simple rule: "When a lead arrives, respond immediately." From there, each layer adds intelligence. Start simple, expand relentlessly.
